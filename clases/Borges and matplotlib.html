(or an introduction to NLP)#

Today’s task is an integration exercise that, if they decide to accept it, is to make a word cloud from one of Borges’s texts found in the Google Drive. XX_MARKDOWN_LINK_XX There are several more stories in case they want to read.

A word cloud is basically a graphic representation of a histogram of words of a text: the most frequent words appear larger. In addition, there is always some design that allows the words to assemble in different orientations so that they appear beautiful.

This is a very simple essay of a relatively new area in computer science, called natural Language Processing, which allows a computer to “understand” (or do as it understands) the spoken language or human written.

def histograma_palabras(lista_unica,texto):
    resultado = {}
    """
    Dada una lista única de palabras y un texto,
    se genera un diccionario conteniendo cada palabra, y la frecuencia
    con la que aparece en el texto
    """
    todas = texto.split(' ')
    for palabra in lista_unica:
        resultado[palabra] = todas.count(palabra)
        
    return resultado
